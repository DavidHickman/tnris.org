Individual Permit Process -- TCEQ 401 Water Certification

Tier II projects are large projects that affect more than three (3) acres of waters of the United States and/or 1,500 linear feet of stream. They also include projects that impact rare and ecologically significant wetlands, after the fact permits, and any projects that either the applicant or Corps determines that Tier I processing is inappropriate. For additional information on 401 Certification see: http://www.tceq.texas.gov/assets/public/permitting/waterquality/attachments/401certification/MOA2.pdf
