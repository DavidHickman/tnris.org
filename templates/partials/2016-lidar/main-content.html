TNRIS has managed more Lidar projects than all other sources combined for the State. In addition to TNRIS-managed projects, we have had a hand in the guidance, specifications and technical expertise for many of the regional and local projects. With the proposed areas of interest (AOI) for 2016-2017, we are committed to providing all state entities a high quality, technically advanced elevation dataset acquisitions with great cost saving and professional management.

The project will be under the umbrella of the the StratMap contract, in cooperation with the Department of Information Resources (DIR). This Stratmap contract, replacing the High Priority Imagery and Data Sets - HPIDS contract, is the second generation of geographic data contracts at TNRIS. Under the HPIDS contract TNRIS managed 19 Lidar acquisitions covering over 50,000 square miles.

The map above represents prospective areas of interest by entities throughout the state. The contract vehicle for these acquisitions will be the StratMap contract residing with DIR. Area of Interest - AOI
Texas Water Development Board - TWDB

  • Funding Status: Committed
  • Area of Interest Status: Preliminary

The Texas Water Development Board has committed $900,000.00 to acquiring high resolution Lidar data for regions of central Texas in an effort to support floodplain management and planning.

These two Areas if interest are the main focus areas for utilization of these funds. Most likely only one of these AOIs will be chosen for acquisition. The deciding factor in supporting one area of interest over the other will hinge on support from local and regional entities to contribute to the acquisition.

Area of Interest - AOI
San Antonio River Authority - SARA

  • Funding Status: Not committed, in need of partners
  • Area of Interest Status: Preliminary

SARA is interested in exploring an acquisition covering parts of their jurisdiction to support activities related to FEMA flood modeling and mapping, feature data extraction, water quality modeling, stream restoration potential analysis, and change detection. Additional local and regional partners are needed to secure funding and to expand the area of interest. This AOI will also seek additional funding from the USGS through the 3DEP program.

Area of Interest - AOI
Williamson County

  • Funding Status: Pending approval
  • Area of Interest Status: Preliminary

Williamson County is interested in acquiring high resolution Lidar data to be used in flood management as well as to assist in transportation planning.

Williamson County is looking for partners to expand the Area of interest in an effort to create a regional dataset consisting of one data specification to improve regional elevation models. This acquisition will match the specification flown for the city of Georgetown to create a seamless dataset using the state Lidar specification.
