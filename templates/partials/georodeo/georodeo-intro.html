Code, Code on the Range

TNRIS invites you to the 3rd annual GeoRodeo - a roundup of geodevelopers and coders from across the state of Texas.

This free, interactive, technical conference, held at the Commons Center of the JJ Pickle Research Campus, is specifically for the Texas GIS/geospatial developer community. It is organized by programmers, for programmers.

Join us to learn about tips and techniques for open data, open source, and commercial GIS applications.
