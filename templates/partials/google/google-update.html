Updates from
August 18, 2015

The transition to the Google Cloud Platform (GCP) is now complete. Unique links are being distributed to current pilot participants and will be completed by August 31st. The new service provides faster response times, the ability to export and print maps, and contains the newest imagery for several areas in Texas.

Google has also cached all imagery down to level 20 (True 6-inch resolution). This provides an extra level of detail in the imagery service.

Please Note: By default, ArcMap version 10.2.2 or prior will only display to level 19. There is a patch that will allow prior versions to consume level 20. The effect on CAD and Microstation users is unknown at this time
